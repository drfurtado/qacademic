Lab 1 - Assessing Locomotor FMS

Purpose

To teach students how to use the FG-COMPASS when assessing Locomotor Fundamental Movement Skills.

Instructions

Step 1

  1. First, read/re-read Furtado Jr & Gallagher (2012);

  2. Visit the FG-COMPASS’ website and study the Testing Protocols for all five locomotor skills;

  3. Get the assessment badge for each of the Locomotor skills of the FG-COMPASS. This means you would be certified to assess the locomotor skills that comprise the locomotor subscale of the FG-COMPASS. This can be done following the instructions provided on Canvas;

  4. Once you’ve earned all five badges (i.e., hop, horizontal jump, skip, vertical jump, gallop), come back to this page and continue with item 5 below;

  5. to be done…
