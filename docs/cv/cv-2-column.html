Research summary

Aims—to develop and evaluate design patterns that help people self-regulate their use of smartphones and laptops.
Methods—controlled studies, user surveys, interviews, co-design workshops, web scraping, behaviour logging.

I am passionate about open and transparent research. Since 2018, I have shared materials, analysis scripts, and data for all my first-authored work, and written my papers as reproducible documents in R Markdown.
